01. About

A historic gathering of
judicial leaders.

아시아 태평양 지역 대법원장들이 사법부의 현대적 과제를 논의하는 최고 권위의 회의입니다.

The Conference of Chief Justices of Asia and the Pacific (CCJAP) brings together the heads of the highest courts from across the region to share experiences and strengthen judicial cooperation.
